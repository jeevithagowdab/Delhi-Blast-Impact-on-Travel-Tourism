

# Delhi Blast Impact on Travel & Tourism – Business & Data Analytics Case Study

This project is a real-world business and data analytics case study analyzing the impact of the Delhi blast on the travel and tourism sector. The project focuses on crisis impact assessment, business recovery strategy, financial forecasting, and investor-style decision support using Power BI.

---

## Project Objective
To understand how a major crisis affects the tourism industry and simulate how a Delhi-based travel startup can recover through data-driven strategy, financial planning, and structured business decisions.

---

## Key Problem Addressed
- Sudden drop in tourist footfall (up to 50%)
- 30–50% hotel and travel booking cancellations  
- Decline in customer confidence and demand  
- Revenue instability for travel businesses  
- Need for investor funding and recovery planning  

---

## What This Project Covers

### Business & Strategy Work
- Impact analysis of crisis on tourism demand  
- Startup recovery strategy (positioning, marketing, funding)  
- Unique value proposition and business direction  
- Investor-style planning and capital allocation  
- 3-year growth and sustainability modeling  

### Data & Analytics Work
- Built a **3-year financial forecast model**
- Defined and tracked key business KPIs:
  - Total Revenue (₹23M forecast)
  - Net Profit (₹6M)
  - Gross Margin & EBIT Margin trend
  - CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio
  - Monthly Active Users growth
  - Break-even timeline
  - Runway after funding (22 months)
- Designed a **Power BI dashboard** to visualize:
  - Financial performance  
  - Growth metrics  
  - Budget allocation  
  - Investor-focused insights  

---

## Dashboard Highlights
The Power BI dashboard includes:
- KPI cards (Revenue, Profit, CAC, LTV, MAU, Break-even)
- Revenue growth trends  
- EBIT margin improvement  
- User growth analysis  
- Funding allocation donut chart  
- Financial summary table for decision-making
